Privacy Policy

AssistMe (“We”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

This policy (together with our terms of use and any other referenced documents) outlines how we collect, process, and store your personal data. Please read carefully to understand our practices regarding your information.

Information We May Collect

  • Information submitted via forms (registration, subscription, contact, etc.)
  • Details of your correspondence when you contact us
  • Survey responses (optional)
  • Order and transaction records
  • Site visit data (traffic, location, communication data, etc.)
  • Response to promotional offers

IP Addresses and Cookies

We may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and OS for analytics and system administration. Cookies help us:

  • Estimate audience size and behavior
  • Customize user experience
  • Speed up searches
  • Recognize returning users

You may disable cookies via your browser, but this may affect your experience on our site. Third-party advertisers may also use cookies beyond our control.

Data Storage & Security

Your data may be stored in India or Canada and processed by our staff or contractors. By submitting data, you agree to this transfer and processing.

We take security seriously, but internet transmission is not completely secure. Data submitted is at your own risk.

Use of Information

  • Deliver requested services or information
  • Improve our website and content presentation
  • Provide marketing (with consent)
  • Fulfill contractual obligations
  • Enable interactive services
  • Notify users of service changes

We may share anonymized data with clients and use it to identify employment or usage trends. If you're concerned about your current employer accessing your data, you may remain anonymous.

Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your data:

  • During business sales, mergers, or acquisitions
  • If legally required
  • To enforce policies or protect rights, safety, and property
  • For fraud prevention and credit risk reduction

Your Rights

You have the right to prevent your data from being used for marketing. You may opt-out via forms or by contacting us anytime at https://assistmehub.com.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites linked from our site. Please review their policies before submitting personal data.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes to our privacy policy will be posted on this page and, if applicable, notified to you via email.

Contact Us

Questions or comments regarding this policy can be sent via our website at: www.assistmehub.com
